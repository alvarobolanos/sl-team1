Transportation in Denver

Whether on foot, by car, by bike, or by light rail, there are plenty of options for getting around Denver. Looking for Downtown Denver transportation to get around the 16th Street Mall? Denver offers free public transportation with the Lightrail. Want to tour the city on a bike? B-Cycle makes Denver even more bike-friendly. These are just a few examples of options for transportation in Denver.





Avis Rent-a-Car

Travel around town in your own rental car. Avis has affordable prices and lots of leg room for families.

Rent a Bycicle

Explore The Mile High City using B-cycle, Denver’s pioneering bike share program. Find out how to set up an account at DenverBcycle.com. You can also download B-cycle’s iPhone app for station locations and bike availability.





Public Transportation

Public transit for metropolitan Denver/Boulder. One-Day and Five-Day Passes are available for unlimited travel for bus and light rail service in the Denver metro area. SkyRide service to/from DIA. Passes can be purchased individually or at a discount for large groups. Please visit our Website for more information.
